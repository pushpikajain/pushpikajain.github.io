About

Biography

Pushpika Jain is an Assistant Professor and Research Scholar in the Department of Pharmacy at KIET Group of Institutions, Ghaziabad. She holds a Master's degree in Pharmacy (Pharmaceutics) from Banasthali Vidyapith and has valuable research experience as a Project Intern in the CBRN department at Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), INMAS, New Delhi. Her research focuses on novel drug delivery systems, particularly in radiation protection and nano-based therapeutic approaches. With expertise in pharmaceutics and a strong foundation in teaching, she is dedicated to advancing pharmaceutical research and education.
